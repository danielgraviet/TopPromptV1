# TopPrompt Marketing Growth Plan

## Positioning
- Best prompt library with live leaderboard + fresh Reddit/HN aggregation aimed at AI practitioners, growth teams, indie builders, and prompt engineers.

## Owned Channels (Automate)
- Weekly "Top 20 prompts" newsletter via Inngest + Resend using cached leaderboard.
- Auto-post top 5 daily to X/LinkedIn via Zapier/n8n using RSS/JSON endpoint.
- Changelog feed (Headway/LaunchNotes) auto-cross-posted to Discord + Slack.

## Outbound Targets (Templatize Outreach)
- AI newsletters: Ben's Bites, TLDR AI, The Rundown, Alignment Newsletter.
- AI creators: Matt Wolfe, All About AI, Data Professor (YouTube/TikTok) with pre-made "5 prompts for X" packs and referral links.
- Community owners: Latent Space, AI Engineers, MLOps.community Slack/Discord — offer read-only "#daily-prompts" bot powered by API.
- Co-marketing: Upstash, Inngest, Resend, and model vendors (OpenAI/Anthropic/Perplexity) for joint case studies.

## Product-Led Growth Loops
- Shareable prompt pages with one-click "Run in ChatGPT/Claude" and social cards.
- Referral boosts: creators gain ranking weight for each signup they refer.
- Embeddable "Top 5 prompts" widget (JS snippet) for AI blogs/tools.
- Themed "Challenge weeks" (e.g., Debugging week) with auto-updating landing pages.

## SEO & Content (Automate)
- Nightly-generated long-tail pages: "Best prompts for <tool/role>" using aggregated data.
- Internal linking job connecting new prompts to related tags/models.
- Schema.org HowTo/CreativeWork markup on prompt pages for rich results.

## Community & UGC
- Monthly prompt competitions; winners featured on homepage + email.
- Creator packs (10–20 prompts) downloadable as JSON/CSV; gate via email capture.

## Activation & Retention
- Save-to-collection plus daily digest for saved categories.
- Webhooks/API for teams to sync saved prompts into Notion/Confluence.
- Browser extension nudge: "Viewed 3 prompts — want today's top 5 emailed?"

## Measurement
- Track: signups by channel, prompt copy clicks, saves/collections, referral conversions.
- Weekly automated growth report (Inngest → CSV → Looker Studio) delivered via email.

## 5 Immediate Steps (Low Effort → High Impact)
1. Launch newsletter + daily social autopost from leaderboard cache.
2. Email 5 AI newsletters with an embeddable "Top prompts of the week" block (UTM-tagged).
3. Ship embeddable widget and offer to 3 AI community sites.
4. Generate 50 SEO pages for "Best prompts for <tool/role>" from existing data.
5. Run a 1-week "Debugging prompt" challenge; feature winners and collect emails.
